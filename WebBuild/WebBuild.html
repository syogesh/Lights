Unity Web Player | Let There Be Light

Let There Be Light is an action, puzzle-platformer where you use mobility to avoid obstacles and escape death.
Help a star purge the darkness and restore light to its once great home!

Instructions:
Move Left/Right - A/D
Jump/Double Jump - W
Teleport - J (while holding direcitons - WASD)
Rewind - K
Reset to last restore point - R (will reset platforms too!)
Toggle Invincibility mode - I (spikes will still kill you)
Skip ahead to various locations:
* Starting Location - 1
* Teleport Altar - 2
* Rewind Altar - 3
* Boss Fight - 4
